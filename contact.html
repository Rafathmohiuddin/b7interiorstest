Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process? +
We start with a comprehensive consultation to understand your vision, followed by space planning, 3D visualizations, material selection, and finally, flawless execution by our expert team.
Do you manufacture your own furniture? +
Yes, through our B7 Sofa Clinic and exclusive collections, we design and manufacture bespoke furniture tailored to fit your space and aesthetic perfectly.
Do you handle commercial projects? +
Absolutely. We have extensive experience designing luxury interiors for shops, restaurants, hotels, and premium office spaces.
How long does a typical project take? +
Timelines vary based on the scope of the project. A single room might take a few weeks, while a full home interior can take 45-90 days. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.
